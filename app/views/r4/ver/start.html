Report maritime emissions for UK MRV

This service is for reporting maritime emissions under the UK’s monitoring, reporting and verification (MRV) scheme.

UK MRV applies to ships over 5,000 gross tonnes, that are transporting cargo and passengers for commercial purposes on voyages:

  • between a UK and non-EEA port
  • between two UK ports
  • within UK ports (including while at berth)

Use this service to:

  • report maritime emissions
  • view documents of compliance
You should continue to use the EU Thetis-MRV service to report maritime emissions for journeys between the UK and the EEA.

You can use the service if you are:

  • a ship operator
  • any organisation or person which has assumed the responsibility for the operation of ships calling at or departing from UK ports
  • an accredited verifier

Before you start

To create an account you’ll need your company details.

Before you can submit emissions data you’ll need to provide details of your vessels, including the IMO number.
